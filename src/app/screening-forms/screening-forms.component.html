THIS DRAFT IS NOT FOR DISTRIBUTION. ITS ONLY PURPOSE IS TO RAISE ISSUES THAT WILL NEED TO BE ADDRESSED DURING PROGRAM IMPLEMENTATION, AFTER THE ORDINANCE IS APPROVED. In accordance with San José Municipal Code (SJMC) Chapter 17.40, the owner of each subject building (or the owner’s authorized agent or design professional ) shall submit this Screening Form.

In most cases, the owner or owner's agent may complete the form. However, if the small building exemption (Section 3) or the exemption due to the absence of a wood-frame target story (Section 4, Question 3) is claimed, those sections and Section 6 must be completed by a California licensed architect, civil engineer, or structural engineer.

Submittal of this Screening Form is required even if the building has completed voluntary seismic strengthening or if the building in its current condition is believed to satisfy the retrofit requirements of SJMC Chapter 17.40.
